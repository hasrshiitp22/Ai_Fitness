Yoga Poses For Your Problem


~Sukhasana

Calms mind, reduces stress, improves breathing, lowers blood pressure, promotes relaxation.

How to Practice Sukhasana:

1. Sit on a mat with legs crossed, knees relaxed.

2. Keep spine straight, shoulders relaxed, and hands on knees (palms up or down).

3. Close eyes, breathe deeply, and hold for 1-5 minutes.

4. Focus on slow, steady breaths to calm the mind.

5. Release gently and stretch legs.

~Child’s Pose(Balasana)

Lowers stress, calms nervous system, reduces heart rate, promotes relaxation, relieves tension.

How to Practice Balasana:

1. Kneel on the mat, big toes touching, knees hip-width apart.

2. Sit back onto heels, exhale as you fold forward.

3. Extend arms forward or rest them alongside your body.

4. Rest forehead on the mat, relax shoulders, and breathe deeply.

5. Hold for 30 seconds to 3 minutes.

~Seated Forward Bend(Paschimottanasana)

Lowers stress, calms mind, improves circulation, reduces heart strain, promotes relaxation.

How to Practice Paschimottanasana:

1. Sit with legs extended straight, feet flexed.

2. Inhale, raise arms, lengthen spine.

3. Exhale, hinge from hips, fold forward.

4. Grasp feet/shins (keep knees slightly bent if needed).

5. Relax head/neck, hold for 30 sec–2 min with steady breathing.

6. Inhale to release slowly.
