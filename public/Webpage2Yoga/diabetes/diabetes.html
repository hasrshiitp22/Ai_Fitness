Yoga Poses For Your Problem


~Dhanurasana

Activates pancreas, regulates insulin, improves digestion, burns fat, boosts metabolism.

How to Practice Dhanurasana:

1. Lie on your belly, legs hip-width apart, arms by sides.

2. Bend knees, hold ankles (not feet) with hands.

3. Inhale, lift chest and thighs, pulling legs up.

4. Keep gaze forward, hold for 15-30 sec while breathing deeply.

5. Exhale, release gently.

~Kapalbhati

Stimulates pancreas, regulates blood sugar, boosts metabolism, detoxifies, improves insulin sensitivity.

How to Practice Kapalbhati:

1. Sit in Padmasana/Sukhasana with a straight spine.

2. Take a deep inhale, then forcefully exhale through the nose (belly pulls in).

3. Let inhalation happen passively (no effort).

4. Repeat quickly (60-100 pumps/min) for 1-3 rounds (30 sec to 1 min each).

5. Pause, breathe normally, observe calmness.

~Vajrasana

Regulates blood sugar, stimulates digestion, boosts insulin sensitivity, calms nerves, enhances metabolism.

How to Practice Vajrasana:

1. Kneel on the floor, keeping knees and feet together.

2. Sit back on your heels, placing palms on thighs.

3. Keep spine straight, shoulders relaxed, and chin parallel to the floor.

4. Breathe deeply, hold for 5-10 mins (ideal after meals).

5. Release gently by leaning forward.
