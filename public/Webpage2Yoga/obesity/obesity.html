Yoga Poses For Your Problem


~Sphinx Pose(Salamba Bhujangasana)

Tones abdomen, stimulates digestion, burns belly fat, strengthens spine, improves metabolism.

How to Practice Salamba Bhujangasana:

1. Lie on your stomach, legs extended, tops of feet down.

2. Place elbows under shoulders, forearms parallel, palms flat.

3. Inhale, gently lift chest, keeping hips and pelvis grounded.

4. Engage core, relax shoulders away from ears.

5. Hold for 5-8 breaths, lengthening the spine.

6. Exhale, lower down slowly.

~Dhanurasana

Burns belly fat, massages digestive organs, strengthens core, boosts metabolism, improves posture.

How to Practice Dhanurasana:

1. Lie on your belly, legs hip-width apart, arms by sides.

2. Bend knees, hold ankles (not feet) with hands.

3. Inhale, lift chest and thighs, pulling legs up.

4. Keep gaze forward, hold for 15-30 sec while breathing deeply.

5. Exhale, release gently.
